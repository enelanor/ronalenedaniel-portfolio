Start Date: February 2024

Designer/Artist Name: Ronalene Daniel, Thomas Luong, Eunseol Park

Project Title: Charge Up Energy Campaign

End Date: March 2024

Total Hours: 25 Hours

VIEW PROJECT

The goal of this project is to create a campaign video for BCIT’s Video Storytelling class. Our team collaborated to produce an ad for a fictional sports drink, Charge Up Energy. We learned to build a story, use filming techniques, and apply sound design, gaining key skills in creating impactful campaign videos.

Brainstorming Process:

During our brainstorming sessions, Thomas introduced the idea of developing a video for a fictional sports drink company called Charge Up Energy. His suggestion inspired the team and set the direction for our project. We collaboratively created a touching story, applied various filming techniques, and integrated sound design to enhance the video's impact. Through teamwork and creative planning, we successfully produced a dynamic and engaging campaign video script with a drawn-out story board.

Concept and Rough Drafts:

The concept for Charge Up Energy started with rough drafts that captured the high-energy sporty vibe we wanted. I pulled together inspiration photos from Pinterest to shape the shots and angles for the video. These images helped set a clear direction and gave us a strong visual framework to work from. With this in place, we refined our drafts, tightening the storyline and bringing our vision for a bold, dynamic campaign to life.

Information Gathering and Image Collection:

The information and image-gathering process for our Charge Up Energy campaign involved collecting visuals that conveyed the vibrant energy we aimed to capture. Thomas designed the logo for the company, which became a key element of our brand identity. As a team, we collaborated on sound design, sourcing non-copyrighted sound effects and selecting background music from YouTube's audio library to enhance the video’s atmosphere. All the footage was filmed by us, allowing us to maintain a consistent look and feel that aligned with our vision for the project.

Content Creation:

The creation process for our Charge Up Energy campaign video was filmed at Coal Harbour Seaside Park in downtown Vancouver, which provided a stunning backdrop for our shots. We all took turns filming but Eunseol took control of most of the shots. After capturing all the footage, we turned to Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, where we pieced together our scenes and refined the flow of the video. I was in charge of the editing process and asked for any feedback or changes I should make from my teammates. To enhance the video experience, we used Adobe Audition to focus on sound design, layering in sound effects and mixing the background music to complement the visuals as a group. This combination of filming and editing tools allowed us to create a polished final product that effectively showcased our fictional brand.
