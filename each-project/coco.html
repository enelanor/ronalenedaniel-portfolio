VIEW PROJECT

The goal of this project is to show what was learned in the Illustrator class while creating wireframes for a website.

Brainstorming Process:

The project began with brainstorming the concept for COCO CAFÉ, a high-end coffee shop. Following this, a style guide was developed to match the concept and target our audience. Inspiration for the site was drawn from other high-end cafes and coffee shops.

Concept and Rough Drafts:

The concept for COCO CAFÉ’s website is to allow users to purchase coffee and read information and facts about coffee. The rough drafts were created using Adobe Illustrator, providing a visual representation of the website layout. Drawing out the wireframes was helpful to visualize the final layout.

Information Gathering and Image Collection:

Information for the website was gathered through research on similar websites. The website content was crafted with the assistance of ChatGPT to engage and excite users about coffee. Images were selected to maintain user engagement and provide a visual understanding of the content.

Project Creation:

Creating the wireframes was manageable due to the thorough planning, although finding the concept was a struggle. Working with Illustrator to create the wireframes was also challenging but proved to be a valuable learning experience.
