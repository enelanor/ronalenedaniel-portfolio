Designer/Artist Name: Ronalene Daniel

Project Title: STUDIO MLD Brandbook

Total Hours: 15 hours

VIEW PROJECT

The goal of this project is to create a personal brand in the student’s Digital Layouts class. The student successfully developed a comprehensive brand book for a dance studio, which serves as a visual and strategic guide for the studio's identity.

Brainstorming Process:

The brainstorming process involved exploring various dance styles and their emotional resonance, leading to the generation of ideas for logos, color schemes, and typography that reflect the studio's identity. Using mind mapping and mood boards, the student visualized their concepts and incorporated peer feedback to refine the branding. The final brand book showcases the studio's logo, color palette, typography, and marketing guidelines, highlighting the student's design skills.

Concept and Rough Drafts:

The concept for STUDIO MLD centers around a streetwear hip-hop vibe, reflecting the energetic and expressive nature of both dance and urban culture. This was inspired by the aesthetics found in streetwear, characterized by bold colors, graphic designs, and a sense of individuality.

The development of STUDIO MLD's brand book involved multiple rough drafts, focusing on creating a bold logo that encapsulates the studio's streetwear hip-hop vibe. The initial drafts began with sketches that explored various concepts, emphasizing unique shapes and styles inspired by urban culture. The goal was to design a logo using Adobe Illustrator, that is not only visually striking but also reflects the energy and individuality inherent in hip-hop dance.

Information Gathering and Image Collection:

The information and image-gathering process for STUDIO MLD involved extensive research on Pinterest, where the student collected a variety of visuals that embodied the desired streetwear hip-hop vibe. By analyzing these images, the student identified themes and elements that resonated with their vision, ensuring that the final designs were both aesthetically appealing.

Project Creation:

The creation process for STUDIO MLD involved turning ideas and inspiration into actual designs. The student started with rough sketches to explore different logo styles, colors, and fonts that matched the studio's streetwear hip-hop vibe. Using InDesign software, these ideas were transformed into digital versions, with adjustments made based on feedback from the instructor. The final designs focused on creating a bold and unified look, resulting in a brand book that clearly represents the identity of STUDIO MLD.
