VIEW PROJECT

This TikTok account was created to help grow an audience and market by promoting May Lene’s YouTube channel through daily posts of short clips from the long-form videos.

VIEW SPREADSHEET

Brainstorming Process:

The idea of creating short-form videos came from the trend among other podcast channels on social media, which was similar to the content produced on May Lene’s channel. Building on that idea, similar content was created and posted on the TikTok account.

Concept and Rough Drafts:

The concept of the videos was to feature short parts from the long-form videos, like highlight clips. Animated text for subtitles was also added to help capture users' attention. The videos followed TikTok and social media trends.

Information Gathering and Image Collection:

All of the clips used for the TikTok account came from May Lene’s YouTube channel, specifically from the series May’s Munchtime. Any additional audio or images were sourced from non-copyrighted materials.

Video Creation:

The clips were taken from the long-form videos, cropped to portrait mode, and subtitles were added using the video editing software CapCut. The clips were then posted daily, following a time schedule on TikTok and using trendy hashtags.
